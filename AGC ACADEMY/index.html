About AGC Academy

Vision, mission, and focus

Vision: To transform lives through quality and result-oriented holistic education.

Mission: To be a reputable education center committed to the empowerment of today's children, inculcating Christian virtues and developing responsible leaders for the church and society.

The school champions academic excellence, disciplined routines, and Christian values so every child grows with confidence, resilience, and a heart for service.
