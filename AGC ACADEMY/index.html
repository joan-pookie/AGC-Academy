About AGC Academy

Vision, mission, and focus

Vision: To transform lives through quality and result-oriented holistic education.

Mission: To be a reputable education center committed to the empowerment of today's children, inculcating Christian virtues and developing responsible leaders for the church and society.

The school champions academic excellence, disciplined routines, and Christian values so every child grows with confidence, resilience, and a heart for service.

Programs Offered

Holistic learning for every stage.

Daycare Services

Safe, loving care for our littlest learners with playful routines grounded in Christian stories and rhythms.

ECD School

Early childhood development where foundational literacy, numeracy, and character habits are introduced joyfully.

CBC Primary Education

CBC-compliant classrooms for Grades 1 to 6 where discovery learning meets discipline, technology, and spiritual formation.
